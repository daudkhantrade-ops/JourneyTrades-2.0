The challenge

Sourcing from Pakistan without someone on the ground is high-risk.

You can't walk the factory floor from abroad. Suppliers overstate capacity, understate lead times, and sometimes deliver production that bears no resemblance to the samples they sent.

Journey Trades operates as your eyes and hands in Pakistan — visiting facilities, verifying documents, coordinating samples, and escalating problems before they become expensive mistakes.

What we do

Six services. One team in Pakistan.

SVC—01

Supplier Discovery

We identify and shortlist manufacturers that match your product, quality, and capacity requirements — drawing on our network and independent research.

SVC—02

Factory Verification

We visit, document, and assess every supplier before you're introduced to them, with a written report and photographs.

SVC—03

Sample Coordination

We manage the full sample cycle: briefing the factory, tracking timelines, reviewing quality on arrival, and shipping to you.

SVC—04

Quality Inspections

We inspect production at key stages and report directly to you — with photographic evidence and a clear pass/fail assessment.

SVC—05

Communication Management

We handle day-to-day communication with your Pakistan suppliers — chasing, escalating, and translating details into clear updates.

SVC—06

Custom Sourcing Projects

We take on complex briefs that require market research, category mapping, or multi-supplier coordination — scoped before any commitment.
